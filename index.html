Finsbury Park Loop
Flat tarmac paths, ideal for beginners
~3.2km · Flat · Parkrun route
Go
Directions: Head north on Lennox Road → enter Finsbury Park at the Oxford Road gate → follow the tarmac path anti-clockwise around the park → one full loop is ~2.4km, add a second half-lap to reach 3.2km → exit back to City North Place. Perfectly flat, well-lit, and you'll share the path with the Saturday parkrun crowd.
🌿
Parkland Walk
Traffic-free nature path, calm and green
~4km out & back · Easy · Nature reserve
Go
Directions: Walk to Finsbury Park station → pick up the Parkland Walk trail entrance on the east side of the park → run north-east along the old railway line (London's longest nature reserve) → turn around at the 2km mark near Crouch Hill → run back. Completely traffic-free, shaded, and beautifully quiet for a mental health run.
🏙️
Highbury Fields Loop
Islington's biggest park, a little more distance
~5km · Gentle hills · Week 5+ recommended
Go
Directions: Head south from City North Place along Blackstock Road → turn left onto Highbury Park → enter Highbury Fields at the north gate → run the perimeter loop (~1.6km) once or twice → head back via Highbury Grove. A step up in distance — save this one for when you're in weeks 5–9 and building towards 5K. There's a free parkrun here every Saturday at 9am too.
