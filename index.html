Your local routes 📍

All starting from City North Place, N4 3FS

Beginner · Weeks 1-4
🌳 Finsbury Park Loop
~3.2km · Flat tarmac · Parkrun route
Head north on Lennox Road, enter park at Oxford Road gate, follow anti-clockwise tarmac path. One loop is 2.4km, add a half lap for 3.2km.
Open in Maps
🏟️ Emirates Stadium Loop
~2.5km · Flat · Iconic landmark
Head south on Blackstock Road, run the marked route around the Arsenal Emirates Stadium. Flat, well-lit, great for early weeks.
Open in Maps
Intermediate · Weeks 3-6
🌿 Parkland Walk
~4km out and back · Traffic-free
Pick up Parkland Walk from Finsbury Park station, run north-east along old railway line, turn at 2km near Crouch Hill, run back.
Open in Maps
🏙️ Highbury Fields Loop
~5km · Parkrun here Saturdays 9am
South on Blackstock Road, left onto Highbury Park, enter Highbury Fields, run the perimeter loop, back via Highbury Grove.
Open in Maps
🏛️ Caledonian Park Loop
~4.5km · Marked running route
Head to Caledonian Park near Caledonian Road tube. 600m marked loop around the Grade II listed clock tower. Repeat laps to hit your distance.
Open in Maps
Advanced · Weeks 6-9
🌊 Clissold Park
~6km · Deer park and ponds
Head east along Green Lanes into Stoke Newington, enter Clissold Park, run the perimeter past the deer enclosure, return via Albion Road.
Open in Maps
🏃 FP to Highbury Fields
~7km · Your 5K race route
Run through Finsbury Park, south along Blackstock Road, loop Highbury Fields, return via Highbury Grove. Save for week 8 or 9.
Open in Maps
🎯 Alexandra Palace via Parkland Walk
~9km · Epic views · End-of-plan run
Take the full Parkland Walk from Finsbury Park to Alexandra Palace. Rising steadily with panoramic London views at the top.
